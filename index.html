Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You will receive an email with instructions to reset your password.

How can I pay my fees online?

Navigate to the Fees Payment section, select your payment method, enter the amount, and follow the instructions to complete your payment securely.

When will my course registration be approved?

Course registration approval typically takes 24-48 hours. If it's been longer, please contact your academic advisor.

How can I access resources for visually impaired students?

All resources are available in accessible formats. Use the resource download section and select your preferred format (text, audio, braille-ready).
